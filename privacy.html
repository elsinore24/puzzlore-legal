Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 9, 2026

Summary: Puzzlore respects your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide game functionality, and we never sell your personal information.

1. Introduction

NDM LABS ("we," "our," or "us") operates Puzzlore (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Game Center Profile: If you choose to sign in with Apple Game Center, we access your Game Center display name and scores for leaderboard functionality.
  • In-App Purchases: Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and improving the App.
  • Analytics: We use Apple's built-in analytics to understand app performance and crash reports. This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Game Progress: Your puzzle progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device.

2.3 Advertising

Puzzlore uses AppLovin MAX to display advertisements. AppLovin and its advertising partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address and general location
  • Ad interaction data
  • Device and app information

For more information about AppLovin's data practices, please visit AppLovin's Privacy Policy.

You can limit ad tracking through your device settings under Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Display your scores on Game Center leaderboards
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve the App and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Apple: For Game Center, in-app purchases, and analytics
  • AppLovin: For advertising purposes (see Section 2.3)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

Your game progress is stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores are stored by Apple Game Center. We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information we process.

6. Children's Privacy

Puzzlore is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Effective Date."
